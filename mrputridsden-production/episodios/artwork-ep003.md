```

Dark dramatic portrait composition, MPD aesthetic — crimson and shadow.

1940s style, holding an electric guitar, powerful and commanding presence.
Lit from above with warm golden light, as if divine. Face clearly visible,
dignified, confident.

BELOW HER, arranged in a row or arc: smaller portraits of the artists
she influenced, each in their era's style —
```
