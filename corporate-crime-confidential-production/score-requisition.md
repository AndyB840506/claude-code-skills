# Score Requisition — Corporate Crime Confidential

No audio-generation tool is connected in this session, so nothing here is a rendered
file — these are creative briefs ready to paste into a generative music tool (Suno, Udio)
or use as search terms against a stock library. Same relationship as the artwork prompts:
written here, executed by you on the actual platform.

---

## 1. Intro cue (~12 sec)

**Brief:** Noir jazz sting. Muted trumpet and upright bass walking a slow minor blues line,
brushed snare, a single moody piano chord landing on the downbeat. Smoky, late-night, 1940s
detective-office atmosphere — not upbeat, not swinging, just tense and cool. Ends on an
unresolved chord that hangs, ready to cut to voice.

**Suno / Udio prompt:**
```
1940s film noir jazz, muted trumpet and upright bass walking bassline, brushed snare,
single moody piano chord, smoky detective office atmosphere, instrumental, no vocals,
tense and unresolved ending, cinematic, 12 seconds
```

## 2. Outro cue (~20 sec)

**Brief:** Same instrumentation as the intro — trumpet, upright bass, brushed snare,
piano — but resolving this time. The walking bassline lands on a settled minor-to-major
cadence, like a case closing. Fades out slowly over the last 5 seconds, brushed cymbal
swell at the very end.

**Suno / Udio prompt:**
```
1940s film noir jazz outro, muted trumpet, upright bass, brushed snare, piano, resolving
minor to major cadence like a case closing, slow fade out with brushed cymbal swell,
instrumental, no vocals, 20 seconds
```

## 3. Transition stinger (~2-3 sec)

Used at every `[TRANSICIÓN]` marker in the script — the audio equivalent of the case-file
tab divider in the written script.

**Brief:** A single low piano/bass hit doubled with a soft filing-cabinet-drawer thud or
paper-rustle texture, short reverb tail. Should feel like a case file being closed and set
down on a desk.

**Suno / Udio prompt:**
```
short cinematic stinger, single low piano and upright bass hit, soft paper rustle and
drawer thud texture layered underneath, short reverb tail, noir detective mood,
instrumental, 2-3 seconds
```

## 4. Background tension bed — Banker segments (loopable, ~60-90 sec)

Sits very low in the mix under Segment 4 (The Banker) narration only.

**Brief:** Sparse, dissonant upright bass drone with occasional soft cymbal swells —
anxious, claustrophobic. No melody, so it doesn't compete with the voice.

**Suno / Udio prompt:**
```
ambient tension bed, sparse dissonant upright bass drone, occasional soft cymbal swells,
no melody, anxious and claustrophobic mood, loopable, instrumental, low in the mix, 90
seconds
```

## 5. Background tension bed — Tolstedt segments (loopable, ~60-90 sec)

Sits very low in the mix under Segment 6 (Tolstedt) narration only — same family as the
banker bed, but colder and more clinical, to mirror the boardroom-vs-bank-floor contrast
built into the visual identity (carbon-blue accent vs. crimson).

**Brief:** Same sparse low-end drone, but add a faint, detuned piano note repeating slowly.
Colder and more clinical than anxious — boardroom, not bank floor.

**Suno / Udio prompt:**
```
ambient tension bed, sparse low upright bass drone, faint detuned piano note repeating
slowly, cold and clinical mood, boardroom atmosphere, loopable, instrumental, low in the
mix, 90 seconds
```
