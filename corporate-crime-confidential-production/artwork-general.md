# Artwork — General Show Cover (Corporate Crime Confidential)

Visual direction: **1940s detective pulp comic**, noir, illustrated (not photorealistic) —
moody dark palette, halftone ink texture. This is the general show identity cover, not a
per-episode "Case File" cover (those get generated per-episode via the artwork workflow,
reusing this same direction).

Brand colors: Ink Black `#141414` · Aged Parchment `#E8DCC4` · Crimson `#8B1A2B`

---

## 1:1 — 3000×3000px (Spotify main cover)

```
1940s detective pulp comic cover illustration, noir style, hand-inked
comic-book look with halftone dot texture like vintage pulp printing —
NOT a photorealistic render.

A detective in a trench coat and fedora sitting at a cluttered wooden
desk under a single hanging lamp, reading an open case file/ledger,
cigarette smoke curling upward into the lamplight. Dramatic hard-edged
shadows, venetian blind light stripes cast across the wall behind him.
Detective positioned in the center-lower two-thirds of the frame.

Moody dark color palette: ink black #141414 background and shadows,
aged parchment #E8DCC4 for lamp glow and highlights, deep crimson
#8B1A2B accent on the lamp light and title lettering.

Text overlay: 'CORPORATE CRIME CONFIDENTIAL' in bold vintage pulp serif
lettering, aged parchment #E8DCC4 with a crimson #8B1A2B drop shadow,
positioned in the top third of the frame. Render text EXACTLY as
written, letter by letter, no changes, no additional words.

Bottom bar: solid ink black #141414 strip, full width. Center: Spotify
logo (circle with three curved lines), small, in silver #A8A8A8.

No extra text outside the specified overlay. Highly detailed illustrated
comic-book style, award-winning pulp cover art, high contrast.
```
