# Artwork — Case File No. 001 (Eight Is Great)

Same visual direction as the general show cover (`artwork-general.md`): 1940s detective
pulp comic, noir, illustrated, halftone ink texture. What changes for an episode cover:
the case file on the desk now carries this episode's number and title, and the overlay
text includes the episode title.

Brand colors: Ink Black `#141414` · Aged Parchment `#E8DCC4` · Crimson `#8B1A2B`

---

## 1:1 — 3000×3000px (Spotify episode cover)

```
1940s detective pulp comic cover illustration, noir style, hand-inked comic-book look
with halftone dot texture like vintage pulp printing — NOT a photorealistic render.

A detective in a trench coat and fedora sitting at a cluttered wooden desk under a
single hanging lamp, reading an open case file/ledger, cigarette smoke curling upward
into the lamplight. The open case file's visible tab reads 'No. 001'. Dramatic hard-
edged shadows, venetian blind light stripes cast across the wall behind him. Detective
positioned in the center-lower two-thirds of the frame.

Moody dark color palette: ink black #141414 background and shadows, aged parchment
#E8DCC4 for lamp glow and highlights, deep crimson #8B1A2B accent on the lamp light and
title lettering.

Text overlay, top third: 'CASE FILE No. 001' small, bold vintage pulp serif, aged
parchment #E8DCC4. Below it, larger: 'EIGHT IS GREAT' in bold vintage pulp serif
lettering with a crimson #8B1A2B drop shadow. Render text EXACTLY as written, letter by
letter, no changes, no additional words.

Bottom bar: solid ink black #141414 strip, full width. Center: Spotify logo, small,
silver #A8A8A8.

No extra text outside the specified overlays. Highly detailed illustrated comic-book
style, award-winning pulp cover art, high contrast.
```

## Technical specs

Same as the general cover (see `artwork-general.md`): 1:1 needs 3000×3000px, JPEG/PNG,
RGB, under 500 KB for Spotify. Generate in Google Flow (labs.google/fx/tools/image-fx),
convert PNG → JPEG and compress via squoosh.app before uploading.
