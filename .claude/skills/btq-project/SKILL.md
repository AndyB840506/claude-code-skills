## 4. Tone Master (TM) — Immovable Rules

### Opening — NEVER abbreviate:
> "Buenas y santas. Feliz día, feliz tarde o feliz noche. Cualquier día que éste sea."

### Episode intro:
> "Esto es Behind the Queue, episodio [NÚMERO]."

### Closing (EP.010+):
> "Esto fue Behind the Queue, episodio [NÚMERO]."
> "Yo soy Andy. Y como diría [personaje]: [frase inspirada]."

### Script markers:
- `[PAUSA]` = 1–2 seconds
- `[PAUSA LARGA]` = 3–4 seconds
- `[REMATE]` = closing line — delivered complete, no softening
- `[NOTA]` = reminder for host, not read aloud
- *cursiva* = voice emphasis

### Data rules:
- **Maximum one data point per script block**, surrounded by Andy's narrative
- Preferred sources: Gallup, McKinsey, Gartner, HBR, Deloitte, SHRM, Salesforce, WEF, PwC

---

## 5. Episode Model (Season 2 — EP.010+)

Each episode uses a videogame, film, song, or anime as entry point, then distills principles of experience, leadership, or human design.

### Standard structure:
1. Hook
2. El puente (natural transition)
3. Los datos (max 1 per block with source)
4. Las lecciones (anchored in cultural reference)
5. Referencias cruzadas
6. Aplicable Hoy (3 concrete actions)
7. Mito o Realidad (minimum 3 rounds)
8. Recomendaciones de Andy
9. Cierre con anécdota (single, third person)
10. Teaser EP siguiente + TM close
