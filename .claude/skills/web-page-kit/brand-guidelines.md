## Voice & Tone

**Personality:** [Conversational / Professional / Bold / Friendly / Expert]

**Do:**
- Speak directly to the reader ("you")
- Be clear and specific
- Use active voice

**Don't:**
- Use jargon or buzzwords
- Be overly formal
- Make claims you can't back up
- Invent results or testimonials

## Design Patterns

### Buttons
- **Primary:** Background: `[accent]`, text: white, padding: 12px 28px, border-radius: 6px
- **Secondary:** Border: `[accent]`, text: `[accent]`, transparent background

### Cards
- Background: white, border: 1px solid `#EEEEEE`, border-radius: 8px, padding: 24px
- Hover: subtle lift (transform: translateY(-2px), box-shadow increase)

### Spacing Scale
16px — 24px — 32px — 48px — 64px — 96px

### Section Rhythm
Alternate backgrounds for visual separation: white / light grey / white
