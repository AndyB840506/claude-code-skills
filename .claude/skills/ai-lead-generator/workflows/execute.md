# Lead Generation Workflow

Ejecutar búsqueda de leads reales y generar reporte.

## Step 1: Understand Requirements

Ask the user:
- What are you selling? (product/service)
- Who is your ideal customer? (industry, size, location)
- What search terms should we use?

## Step 2: Execute Real Searches

Use WebSearch or Serper API to find:
- Companies in target industry
- Decision makers/contacts
- Recent activity or signals

Never fabricate leads. Every result must come from actual search.

## Step 3: Score Each Lead (0-100)

Evaluate based on:
- **Relevance:** Does company match target? (0-30 points)
- **Intent signals:** Active hiring, funding, new products? (0-30 points)
- **Accessibility:** Can we find contact info? (0-20 points)
- **Fit:** Company size and stage match? (0-20 points)

## Step 4: Categorize by Probability

Based on score:
- **PREMIUM:** 80-100 (ready to contact)
- **HOT:** 60-79 (high probability)
- **WARM:** 40-59 (medium probability)
- **COLD:** 0-39 (research needed)

## Step 5: Generate HTML Report

Create professional report with:
- Company name, website, contact
- Lead score and category
- Why this is a good fit
- Recommended next step

Export as:
- HTML (viewable in browser)
- PDF (shareable)
- CSV (import to CRM)

## Output

✓ Real leads with verified info  
✓ Individual scores (0-100)  
✓ Probability categories  
✓ Professional reports  
✓ Multiple export formats
