Understanding Kuma Group's Two Platforms

A plain-language guide — no technical knowledge required
Platform 1 of 2

Kuma Flow

Never miss a lead, even at 2 in the morning.
Think of it this way
Imagine you hired the world's most reliable receptionist. She never sleeps, never takes a coffee break, and never forgets to follow up. The moment someone shows interest in your business — whether they sent a text, a Facebook message, an Instagram DM, or an email — she responds within 10 seconds. She asks the right questions to figure out what they need, and she keeps following up politely until they either become a customer or clearly walk away. That receptionist is Kuma Flow.
  • Responds to every inquiry instantly — even at 2 in the morning, on weekends, and on holidays. A potential customer who reaches out never gets silence.
  • Asks smart questions to find out who's serious — not just "hi, how can I help?" but the right questions for your business, so you only spend time on leads worth your attention.
  • Follows up automatically until the lead converts or goes cold — sends a gentle reminder the next day, then again a few days later, then checks back after a month. All without you lifting a finger.
Real-world example

A flooring company runs a Facebook ad. On Monday morning, 40 homeowners have clicked it and sent messages asking for quotes — most of them at 10 PM the night before. Without Kuma Flow, the owner would spend all Monday morning just reading and replying. With Kuma Flow, every one of those 40 people got a warm, professional response within 10 seconds of clicking. By Monday morning, 18 of them have already answered the qualifying questions. The owner spends 30 minutes calling the 6 best leads — not 4 hours reading messages.

Who it's for Any small business that spends money on ads or social media to attract customers — but then loses those customers because no one was available to respond quickly enough. Contractors, service businesses, retailers, clinics, restaurants, real estate agents, and anyone else whose customers might reach out at any hour.
Platform 2 of 2

Hiresignal

Your first-round interview, done automatically by text message.
Think of it this way
Imagine you could run a first-round interview with every single person who applied to your job — without spending a minute on the phone, without scheduling anything, and without reading a single resume. Each applicant gets a short, friendly text conversation that asks them the important questions: Are you available? Do you have the right experience? What are you looking for? The ones who answer well and fit what you need get flagged for you. The ones who don't, get filtered out. That conversation is Hiresignal.
  • Texts every applicant automatically — the moment someone applies to your job posting, Hiresignal sends them a short screening conversation via SMS or WhatsApp. No app. No login. Just a text.
  • Asks your specific questions and scores the answers — you decide what matters (availability, certifications, experience, start date). The AI scores each applicant against your requirements.
  • Gives you a ranked shortlist, not a pile of resumes — you open your dashboard and see the top 5 candidates, each with a summary of their conversation. You call the ones worth calling.
Real-world example

A restaurant in Mississauga, Ontario posts a job for two line cooks. By the end of the week, they have 200 applications. Normally, this would mean reading 200 resumes and playing phone tag for days. With Hiresignal, every applicant got a text screening conversation automatically. Of the 200, only 8 answered correctly, were available for the right shifts, and had relevant experience. The owner called those 8, interviewed 4, and hired 2 — all in two days, without reading a single resume.

Who it's for Any business that hires regularly and spends too much time on resumes, phone screens, and no-shows. Restaurants and hospitality, retail, healthcare support, construction and trades, logistics, childcare — anywhere that hiring is frequent, time-consuming, and expensive.
