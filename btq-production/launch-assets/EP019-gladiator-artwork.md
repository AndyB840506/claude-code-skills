## PORTADA 1:1 — (generar en Stage C)

Concepto: el referente PRESENTE en escena (regla 5 de la dirección) — Máximo, UN gladiador
solo, de pie en el centro de la arena del Coliseo, sobrevivió la pelea, sword down. La
imagen de "el que se quedó de pie, solo, cuando le quitaron todo".

```
Square podcast cover, 1:1, 3000x3000px. Graphic editorial poster design
with strong backlit silhouettes — NOT photorealistic 3D. The subject reads
as a pure black shape with thin golden rim light (#C9A84C) on its edges,
against a wall of warm golden stage light (#C9A84C) with haze and smoke
forming a glowing halo. NO visible face detail — solid silhouette and rim
light only. Era-correct armor and props for ancient Rome (~180 AD). Analog
film grain, slight halation on the lights, muted retro color grading,
screen-print poster texture. No modern elements.

center of the Colosseum arena floor, seen from slightly below so he looms.

segmented armor and a crested helmet reading only as edges of golden light,
head level. The pose is stillness after the fight — not mid-swing.
Around and above him, the tiered stands of the Colosseum dissolve into
darkness, filled with thousands of tiny out-of-focus golden lights
(torches / the roaring crowd), very dim, forming a bowl of light around the
lone figure. Count check: one person total.

BACKGROUND: void black #0A0A0A dominates the top and bottom of the image.

TYPOGRAPHY (render text EXACTLY as written, letter by letter, no changes):

    Line 1: "GLADIATOR" — huge ultra-bold condensed sans-serif, uppercase,
            signal gold #C9A84C
    Line 2: "LA HUELLA QUE DEJA UN LIDER" — white, uppercase, same font,
            one third the size of Line 1, letter-spaced

FOOTER: full-width strip at the very bottom, on the void black background.
  Left: "Behind the Queue" in small white sans-serif
  Center: "EP.019" in bold signal gold #C9A84C
  Right: small silver platform icons — Spotify (circle with three curved
  lines) and YouTube (rounded rectangle with play triangle)
```

**Checklist:** (1) EXACTAMENTE 1 silueta de gladiador — si Flow mete más figuras o público
