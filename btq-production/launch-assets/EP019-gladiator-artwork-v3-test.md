## PORTADA 1:1 — 3000×3000px

```
Dark cinematic editorial image, rendered with real volume and material
texture (muscle, armor, fabric) — NOT a flat silhouette cutout. Void
black background #0A0A0A.

Center composition: a Roman gladiator — Máximo — seen from behind or
at a three-quarter angle, standing at the edge of the arena looking
out past the gates toward the exit. His gladius rests point-down at
his side, not raised. Posture calm and reflective — a leader who has
already won, thinking about what he leaves behind, not fighting.
Segmented armor and helmet rendered with real material shading —
metal, leather straps, worn fabric — not a flat black shape.

Dark, moody shading with warm golden #C9A84C rim light and ambient
glow outlining his figure, like a dark statue lit from behind.

Background: concentric circles of thin gold #C9A84C line art radiating
behind him like a halo, framed by a Roman meander/key border pattern
(the same geometric guilloche style used in Roman mosaics) — subtle,
like an engraved watermark, not competing with the figure.

No circuit boards. No cartoon style. Cinematic.

Typography:
- Top center: five small gold #C9A84C dots
- "BEHIND THE QUEUE" — ultra bold white condensed sans-serif, large
- "BTQ" — small gold below

Below the figure:
"Gladiator: la huella que deja un líder"
White text, bold

Footer black bar at bottom:
- Left: "Behind the Queue" white
- CENTER: "EP.19" gold #C9A84C — prominent
- Right two rows:
  Row 1: Facebook icon, Instagram icon, TikTok icon
  Row 2: Spotify icon, Apple Podcast icon, Amazon Music icon

Do NOT place any text in the bottom-right corner.
Format: square 3000x3000px.
```

**Checklist:** (1) EXACTAMENTE 1 gladiador — no inventar una segunda figura que no viene al
caso en esta historia; (2) render con volumen/textura real (músculo, metal, cuero) + luz
dorada de contorno — NO silueta plana lisa; (3) círculos concéntricos + greca romana de
fondo, finos, sin competir con el texto; (4) tipografía en las 3 capas (puntos, wordmark,
BTQ) + el título debajo de la figura; (5) footer con las 2 filas de íconos completas; (6)
texto letra por letra.
